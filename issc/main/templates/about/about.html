Purpose of the System

The Integrated Campus Safety and Security System (ICSSS) is developed to strengthen campus safety by combining facial recognition technology with an efficient, web-based incident reporting platform. The system ensures that only authorized individuals can be identified and monitored through advanced facial recognition, while also providing students, faculty, and staff with a simple and accessible way to report incidents. By simplifying both identity verification and safety communication, the ICSSS supports a more secure, transparent, and responsive campus environment.
